THE SOLANA FELLAS

What are the Fellas? The Fellas are a Solana-based NFT collection, created with a verifiably random generative algorithm. They were uniquely designed to establish a subcommunity in the Solana space for people to connect and bond over their Fellas.

What is Solana?

Solana Blockchain: The Solana blockchain is a cheaper and faster alternative to Ethereum. It's gas fees are minimal and the efficiency of the network is lightning fast.

  • Fast Transfers
  • Incredible Connectivity
  • Seamless configurations
  • NFT's Visible in your wallet

All of these things make Solana the superior blockchain for a beginner or advanced NFT collector, saving them thousands of dollars in gas fees.

Why Solana Fellas?

The Fellas... The Solana Fellas are a generative collection of NFTs that are perfect for profile pictures, trading, collecting, and more. Each of them are unique with verifiably rare characteristics, making them more valuable amongst collectors.
